# Further Reading

Books, standards, and online resources to take you beyond Bolton.

## Books

### Beyond the basics
- **Hugh Jack** — *Automating Manufacturing Systems with PLCs* (free PDF, comprehensive)
- **Frank Petruzella** — *Programmable Logic Controllers* (US-style, complementary to Bolton)
- **Daniel Kandray** — *Programmable Automation Technologies*

### Specific manufacturers
- **Siemens** — *Automating with SIMATIC S7-1500* (Hans Berger)
- **Allen-Bradley** — *Logix 5000 Controllers Programming Manual* (free from Rockwell)

### Safety
- **Charles Fryman** — *Functional Safety for Industrial Plants* (IEC 61511 focus)
- **HSE UK** — *Out of Control* (free download — case studies of control system failures)

## Standards (the canonical sources)

| Standard | Topic |
|----------|-------|
| IEC 61131-3 | PLC programming languages |
| IEC 60204-1 | Safety of machinery — electrical equipment |
| IEC 62061 | Functional safety of machinery (SIL) |
| ISO 13849-1/2 | Safety-related parts of control systems (PL) |
| IEC 61508 | Functional safety (general — process industries) |
| IEC 61511 | Functional safety — process industry sector |
| EN 12952 / EN 12953 | Water-tube and shell boilers |
| NFPA 79 | Electrical standard for industrial machinery (US) |
| NFPA 85 | Boiler and combustion systems hazards (US) |

## Online Resources

- **PLCdev** (plcdev.com) — manufacturer-agnostic tutorials
- **Automation Direct's videos** — vendor-led but well-paced
- **PLC Programming.org** — community Q&A
- **r/PLC** subreddit — practical war stories
- **Siemens Industry Online Support** — even if you don't use Siemens, the depth is incredible
- **Rockwell Knowledgebase** — same for Allen-Bradley

## Free Simulators and Tools

- **OpenPLC** — open-source soft-PLC, great for learning
- **CODESYS Trial** — full IDE for 30 days
- **TIA Portal Trial** — Siemens
- **Studio 5000 Logix Emulate** — Allen-Bradley simulator
- **Factory IO** — 3D simulated factories you can control with a real or simulated PLC

## YouTube Channels

- **RealPars** — bite-sized PLC concept videos
- **Tim Wilborne** — practical Allen-Bradley tutorials
- **Industrial Automation** — broad coverage

## Communities

- **PLCtalk.net** forum — long-running, experienced practitioners
- **/r/PLC** — Reddit community
- **Stack Overflow** — has a PLC tag for code-level questions
- **LinkedIn groups** for specific platforms (Siemens, AB, etc.)

## Career-adjacent topics

If you want to grow beyond pure PLCs:

- **HMI / SCADA** — Ignition, FactoryTalk View, WinCC
- **Industrial networking** — PROFINET, EtherNet/IP, OPC UA
- **Motion control** — multi-axis coordinated motion (Beckhoff TwinCAT, Allen-Bradley CIP Motion)
- **MES / IIoT** — moving up the automation pyramid
- **Functional safety engineering** — TÜV-certified courses (TÜV Rheinland, TÜV SÜD)

## Specifically for Danish learners

- **Mercantec / DTU** offer further automation courses
- **DI Automation** trade group hosts events
- The Danish-language *Automatik* magazine covers industry news
- **CELF** and other technical schools offer Siemens TIA Portal certification
